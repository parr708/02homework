Where I Live

Frazier Park is located approximately 45 miles north of Los Angeles. My wife and I bought a house here this past summer. Frazier Park experiences all four seasons. We've enjoyed our first winter season and the heaviest snow in 35 years.

Nebraska

I am originally from Nebraska. We Nebraskan's are die hard Husker fans. I am proud to share my grandfather was the youngest player to play for Nebraska at 16 years old! When my grandfather passed away my grandmother went back to school at University of Nebraska and I am proud to say is the oldest Husker to graduate at 83!

Hobbies

I am a very physically active person. I like to walk, run, and be physically active. One of my favorite activities is disc golf. Not everyone knows what disc golf is. It's like golf with frisbees. You have 18 holes and fly a frisbee into the basket. You try to get it in the fewest shots possible. I like it so much I bought my own basket and have it in my front lawn to play H-O-R-S-E with friends and family.
